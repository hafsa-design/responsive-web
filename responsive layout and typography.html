Founding and Philosophy

Erased Tapes Records was established with an idea of assisting in creative, modern classical musicians. The label focuses on freedom of creation and experimental sound design, creating a space of artists and listeners.

Key Artists

Among notable artists Nils Frahm, Ólafur Arnalds and A Winged Victory for the Sullen can be named. Their music incorporates minimalism, ambient and electronic music in order to create distinctive emotional scenery.

Live Performances

The label also specialises in live shows which is a combination of visual arts and music. This strategy offers the audience with the complete experiences that are more than traditional concerts.
